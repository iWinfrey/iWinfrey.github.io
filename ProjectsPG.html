Rock Paper Scissors!

Here is a classic pong game that I used JavaScript as the code. I had to watch to tutorial video from Udemy to make it. It's slightly different than the way they have it and I might make some other additions to the game in some time. Until then, the core gameplay works and so does the scoring system, so please enjoy. The game ends after one side reaches 3 points. When the game is over, you will be prompted to click the screen to restart it, otherwise just hit the back button or go to another page to leave.
